FAVBET Supports the Program “Implementation in Ukraine of Social Services for People at Risk of Gambling Addiction” by the Responsible Gambling Center

Qualified Support for Players Seeking to Restore Balance in Their Lives

As a socially responsible company, FAVBET has become the General Partner of a comprehensive project to implement social services in Ukraine for people at risk of gambling addiction. In 2024, as part of this project and with support from partner companies, the first branch of Life Space opened in Ukraine — a specialized center providing assistance to players and their families in overcoming gambling addiction and promoting a culture of responsible gaming.

Created through effective collaboration among the public, government, and business sector, Life Space marks an important milestone in building a comprehensive system to prevent gambling addiction in Ukraine. This system encompasses educational outreach, medical assistance, and support for reintegration into society.

About the Life Space Center

Life Space is a social and psychological support center established by the Responsible Gaming Cente as part of a joint project with the Ministry of Social Policy of Ukraine, supported by responsible Ukrainian businesses and partner companies.

It is a safe space for players and their families where they can receive professional assistance and the necessary resources to return to a fulfilling life. The Center's specialists focus not only on timely diagnosis but also on developing individualized recovery programs that promote psychological change and build strong motivation for recovery.

In addition to psychological support, the Center offers legal consultations regarding inclusion in the Register of Persons Restricted from Access to Gambling Establishments, as well as assistance with a range of other related issues.

Regular scientific and educational projects are also held here, aimed at preventing gambling addiction and raising public awareness of its risks.

At Life Space, we understand the sensitive nature of gambling addiction issues, so all services are provided with confidentiality and respect for visitors' personal lives.

Services and Resources

Life Space is always open to people who wish to regain control over their habits, their relatives and loved ones, as well as community activists interested in issues related to preventing gambling addiction.

    The Center's services include:
  • Detailed diagnosis of the causes and extent of addiction
  • Development of personalized recovery plans
  • Psychological training to strengthen internal resources and increase motivation for recovery
  • Support in reintegration into society and guidance on preventing relapse into gambling addiction
  • Educational activities to raise awareness about addiction problems, including lectures and informational campaigns for the public

Social Significance

Life Space is one of the first examples in the history of Ukraine's gambling market where business, the state, and the public have united against a problem that has remained hidden for many years.

While gambling entertainment does carry a risk of addiction, years of international experience show that this risk can be minimized with sensible regulation, consideration of players' interests, and open dialogue among all involved parties.

FAVBET will continue to support government initiatives and joint projects aimed at protecting the health and well-being of players. At the same time, we remain committed to implementing effective self-control mechanisms on our own platforms to protect users from the risks of gambling addiction.

We urge everyone facing the challenges of gambling addiction not to isolate themselves but to seek professional and effective help. Life Space is ready to become your reliable guide on the path to a better quality of life. Responsible gaming begins with a responsible choice, so don’t hesitate to seek support.
